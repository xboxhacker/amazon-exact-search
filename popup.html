🔍 Exact Amazon Search

This extension will:

  • Filter is off until you click Search or Filter
  • Unquoted words must each appear in the title
  • Prefix with - to omit titles with that word (e.g. -4mm, -M4)
  • Quoted phrases like "4mm OD" must appear exactly together
  • Strict size matching (M6 won't match M4, M5, or M60)
  • Click Remove Filter on Amazon to turn it off again
